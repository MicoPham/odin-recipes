3-ingredient Mac & Cheese

Ooey, gooey, and oh-so-delightful. Here is a very easy 3-ingredient mac-hack.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups milk
  • 1 lb elbow macaroni, uncooked
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Optional Ingredients

Add any of these in any amounts to your liking.
  • cream cheese
  • heavy cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • salted butter

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and stir constantly until the pasta is cooked, about 8 minutes.
  3. Turn off the heat, then add the cheddar. Stir until the cheese is melted and the pasta is evenly coated.
  4. Add sugar, salt, black pepper, milk and mix
  5. Enjoy!
